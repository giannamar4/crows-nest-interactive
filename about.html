About Us

Founded in 2022 and based in Phoenix, Arizona, Crows Nest Interactive is a game developing company distributing games on Steam. As of now, we are a team of two splitting the work of art design and programming. All of our products are independently made and unique to our company. We aim to provide creative, enjoyable horror stories through games that are fun and affordable.

Our passion for games has led us on this journey and are excited for the future. We are on our first year of development and have several projects lined up. Thank you for all of the support!
